#  Page not found

Sorry, it seems you were trying to access a page that doesn't exist.

Please check the spelling of the URL you were trying to access and try again.

**We have just moved to a new website. To view our old site visit:**

[ http://archive.stats.govt.nz ](http://archive.stats.govt.nz "archive
website")

**If you want to visit an exact page that was on our old site:**

  1. Replace the 'www' part of the url with 'archive' 
  2. Make sure the start says 'http' NOT 'https' 

As an example:

**https** :// **www** .stats.govt.nz/methods/classifications-and-
standards/classification-related-stats-standards/main-means-of-travel-to-
work.aspx

becomes

**http** :// **archive** .stats.govt.nz/methods/classifications-and-
standards/classification-related-stats-standards/main-means-of-travel-to-
work.aspx

If you are still having difficulty, please contact us via live chat, or our [
contact form ](/contact-us/) .
